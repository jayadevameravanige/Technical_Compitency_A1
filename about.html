About AGRO CYCLE

Learn about our mission, technology, and the team behind the Crop Yield Prediction project.

Our Mission

AGRO CYCLE is dedicated to empowering farmers with cutting-edge technology to enhance agricultural productivity and sustainability. Our mission is to democratize access to advanced predictive analytics for all farmers, regardless of the size of their operations.

Through the integration of machine learning and agricultural science, we aim to help farmers make data-driven decisions, optimize resource utilization, and mitigate risks associated with weather variations and other environmental factors.

Our Technology

The heart of AGRO CYCLE is our advanced machine learning model that has been trained on extensive agricultural datasets from various regions, crops, and growing conditions. This model integrates multiple data points including:

  • Weather Data: Temperature, rainfall, humidity, wind speed, and seasonal variations.
  • Soil Analysis: pH levels, nutrient content (N, P, K), organic matter, and soil type.
  • Crop-Specific Factors: Growth stages, historical yield data, variety characteristics, and disease resistance.
  • Geographic Information: Altitude, terrain, and local climate patterns.

Our algorithm continuously learns and improves as more data becomes available, ensuring that our predictions become increasingly accurate over time.

Project Story

AGRO CYCLE began as a research project at GM University, Davangere, with the goal of addressing the challenges faced by farmers in predicting crop yields in the face of changing climate patterns and increasing environmental unpredictability.

Our team of agricultural experts, data scientists, and software engineers collaborated to develop a user-friendly platform that can translate complex agricultural data into actionable insights for farmers.

What started as an academic endeavor has now evolved into a comprehensive tool that's being used by farmers across different regions to optimize their farming practices and improve yields.

Our Team

AGRO CYCLE is developed by RAJN TEAM, a group of dedicated researchers and developers from GM University. Our multidisciplinary team brings together expertise in:

  • Agricultural Science: Deep understanding of crop physiology, farming practices, and growth factors.
  • Data Science: Expertise in machine learning algorithms, statistical analysis, and predictive modeling.
  • Software Development: Skills in creating intuitive, user-friendly interfaces and robust backend systems.
  • Climatology: Knowledge of weather patterns and their impact on agricultural outcomes.

We are committed to continuous improvement and welcome feedback from farmers and agricultural experts to enhance our platform.
